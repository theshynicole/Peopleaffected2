People Affected

Many different communities were affected by the pandemic, which resulted in many groups being affected in many different. As a result, someones experience during COVID-19 might look different from someone elses perspective. Here are some of the groups that were affected during this time.

Person with disabilities had to face many issues such as not being able to access healthcare, be more at risk of catching COVID-19 and facing discrimination due to their disabilities.
Many youths during this time also had to deal with isolation and not being able to see their own friends. As well, many young people during this time did not have the access to many resources that would be accessible in people such as mental health services and teacher help.
Indigenous people at this time are also heavily affected due to the lack of resources and aid that are given to them, especially when they are needed it the most.
Many young families were also affected by COVID-19 and are still not able to access basic services to provide for their young ones.
